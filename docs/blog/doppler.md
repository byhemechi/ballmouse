### Doppler Effect equation

$$
f' = f\frac{v_{wave} \pm v_{observer}}{v_{wave}\pm v_{source}}
$$

$$
\text{Sound} = 340ms^{-1}
$$

$$
\text{Light} = 3\times10^8ms^{-1}
$$

#### Examples
1)

$$
\begin{aligned}
f' &= 650 \frac{320ms^{-1}}{320ms^{-1}- 20ms^{-1}} \\
f' &= 693.\overline{3}
\end{aligned}
$$
